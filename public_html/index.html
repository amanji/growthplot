Watch your child grow!



An easy way to take your child's measurement information and tack their growth over time. Your health care worker uses your child's measurements such as height, length, and head circumference (for children under 2) to ensure that he or she is growing normally for their age. It is one of the signs of good general health and is used by health care professionals as a screening tool. You can also use these growth measuremnts, regardless of where it is obtained to record and plot your child's growth and access their growth percentiles over different points in time. The information can be used for your own records or shared with a health care professional if you choose. This program uses data from the World Health Organization Growth standards and refereces and plots it on WHO growth curves. For more information on growth curves please see whogrowthcharts.ca
