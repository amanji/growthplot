Your health care worker uses your child's measurements such as height/length, weight and head circumference(under 2) to ensure that he/she is growing in an appropriate manner. It is one of the signs of good general health and is used as a health screening tool.


You can also use these growth measurements, regardless of where it is obtained, to keep track of their numbers and even obtain their percentiles at different points over time.


The information can be used for your own personal information and/or to share with your health care team.


This program uses the data from the World Health Organization Growth standards and references and plots it on the set 2 WHO growth curves. For more information on growth curves and measurements see whogrowthcharts.ca
